Usage Modes
You can choose among the given below modes to continue:

Pay and verify

In this mode, use of our application is metered. You will be charged whenever you want to use our application. If you prefer this mode, then you can verify only one document per pay.

Payment

Purchase Credit and verify

This mode supports deferred payment. If you prefer this usage mode, then you have to purchase credits. Then you can verify your document while spending credits.

Monthly Subscription
Subscribe monthly and verify

In this mode, you need to subscribe on monthly basis. After subscribing, you can verify as many as documents during that month. After the period is over, you need to subscribe again or switch to other usage mode.
